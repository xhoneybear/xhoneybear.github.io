Who am I?

                        
Hola! My name is Camille, I'm  and I am a medical informatics student at Wrocław University of Science and Technology! I'm a science person, hugely interested in IT, nuclear physics and chemistry, medicine and biology, especially genetics. Aside from that my passions also include singing, digital art and cooking!

                    

                    

                        
What I do

                        
I code out of passion! Tech is something I have been keen on for a really long time and I've grown with it, starting my journey with installing custom Android ROMs and burning Linux Mint CDs when I was around 10 or 11. Now I mostly code in Python, Bash, Scala and HTML/CSS & JS and strive to make exciting projects!

                    

                    

                        
My setup

                        
My primary device is a laptop so I can code on the go! I am a UNIX-like OS fan with macOS-like flow so I'm currently using Arch Linux with GNOME (with an intent to maybe invest in a MacBook later on). I use VS Code for most of my programming stuff and occasionally Android Studio for building apps for mobile.

                    

                    

                        
My profile

                        
I'm openly learning to code and I am not specialized in any field! I am an aspiring front-end but I am also capable of backend and scripting. I am a unicorn - I'm a creative mind preferring to experiment and be original in my approach. I also have a huge sense of aesthetics and design everything with utmost care.
